    • FestivArt's dedication is to provide a space to recognize and appreciate student artworks from all over the Philippines. As fellow students, we acknowledge the lack of support for young local artists. Therefore, we aim to instigate a movement that shows how art made by these artists is significant in promoting local culture and artistry. We welcome and feature artists that engage with all kinds of art forms and take pride in creating beyond the restrictions of traditional arts.
